The Best Manga According to Me

My top 3 Manga of all-time.

Fuuka

A Boy whose life changes after meeting a mysterious girl, leading to love, music, and heartbreaking twists.

Berserk

A scarred mercenary consumed by vengeance and haunted by his anguished, unbreakable love for his beloved,
as he battles relentless demonic horrors in a cruel, unforgiving world.

One Piece

A spirited pirate captain chasing his dream of becoming Pirate King, his heart tethered to his crew and a fleeting,
painful love for his beloved, as he sails a vibrant yet treacherous world.
